Complete Systems

COMPLETE WATER-FED POLE SYSTEMS - VEHICLE OR TRAILER MOUNTED

We can design a unique system to suit your individual requirements - but as a start point - a typical pure water window cleaning system would have a 500lt in-vehicle water tank, and be set-up to run 2 water-fed poles concurrently, allowing 2 operators to be cleaning at the same time.

The system can be installed into a variety of vehicles - for example a VW Caddy Panel Van or similar; most 1-Ton single cab bakkies; some double-cab bakkies; a VW Combi panel van; or a Kia K2700 1.3T flat-bed or similar.

In fact we can design a system to suit most vehicles, but the limitation in each case is usually the payload of the vehicle i.e. how much weight is the vehicle designed to carry? (Remember that 1000lt of water weighs one Ton - so you can't carry that on a half-ton bakkie!).

Vehicle System

A typical system would include all of the following items:

  • A complete static pure water production unit - comprising a 400GPD Reverse Osmosis unit, 1000lt storage tank, submersible transfer pump & delivery hose
  • The complete 2-line vehicle or trailer installation - complete with DI vessels, pumps, strainers, split battery charger and deep-cycle battery, flow controllers, and metal hose reels with 100m micro-bore hose
  • A 500lt baffled in-vehicle water tank (can be 1000lt depending on the size of the vehicle)
  • 2 x complete telescopic Water-Fed Poles - 2.8m & 9.4m - complete with jetted brushes, pole hose, adjustable goosenecks & connectors
  • A hand-held digital TDS meter for you to easily monitor your water quality
  • 2 sets of window squeegees, window mops, telepole and scrapers - for internal window cleaning
  • Full installation into your vehicle (both mechanical and auto-electrical installation)

The systems that we supply and install are exactly the same as we have used in our own fleet of service vehicles over the past 10 years.

You deliver your vehicle to us - and we give it back fitted with a fully-tested and proven pure water window cleaning system - ready to go and work!





Trailer System

All of the equipment that gets fitted into the vehicle above can be fitted into a custom built trailer.

We do not manufacture the trailer but rather put you in touch directly with the trailer manufacturer so that you receive a trailer that meets your needs exactly.

Height of work

The poles included in the typical system allow you to reach up to a height of 3-storeys, and should you intend to do work at 4th and 5th-storey height you will require a carbon-composite or a full carbon fibre pole.

Quality Products

All of the STREAMLINE products used in our systems carry a full one year's warranty according to the manufacturers' standard terms and conditions of sale.

Training & Support

We offer hands-on training on the use and maintenance of all products purchased from us. This training is provided at our premises in Florida Park (JHB) and in the field - and is included in our system prices.

Spares

We carry a comprehensive range of critical spares in stock to support all products that we sell. These spares are held at our premises in Florida Park (JHB) and can usually be sent by over-night courier to most locations within South Africa.

Delivery time

The delivery time on our systems vary between 1 and 3 weeks - dependant on the final configuration of your chosen system.
