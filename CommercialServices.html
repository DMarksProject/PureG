Commercial Window Cleaning Services

Services

Our commercial window cleaning services are tailored to meet our client's specific requirements and range from one-off and pre-occupational cleans to regular service contracts, for all types of windows and glass - both externally and internally.

We also offer a specialized cleaning service for building cladding, facades and all types of signage.

The advantages of our method are:

  • Safety – no ladders or scaffolding are required
  • High Reach – up to 5-storeys from the ground
  • Cleaner – leaves windows with no spotting or streaking
  • Fast – up to 70% quicker than traditional methods
  • Cost-effective – competitive pricing due to the speed of our method

Also, as no chemicals or detergents are used in the process, WFP systems are 100% environmentally friendly, providing the perfect solution to your “GREEN” window cleaning needs.

Accreditation

We are fully BEE compliant and are currently in good standing with both SARS and The Compensation Fund. (All Certificates etc. are available for inspection should this be a bona fide requirement).
