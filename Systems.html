About Company Page


We offer professional window cleaning services for all types of properties (both residential & commercial) and we supply products and complete systems for the pure water cleaning industry.


High reach window cleaning systems utilizing Water Fed Pole technology for domestic and commercial window cleaning were first introduced in Europe in 1996, and Pureglaze pioneered the introduction of this unique service in South Africa. Safety regulations strictly govern the conditions under which working at height can now be undertaken, making the use of ladders for window cleaning increasingly more difficult. The use of traditional window cleaning methods is declining rapidly as safety regulations force the switch to Water Fed Pole Systems.
